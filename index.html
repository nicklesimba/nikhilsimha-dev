A bit about me

I'm a platform and infrastructure engineer based in Austin, TX. Right now I'm a backend engineer at a fintech clearing and investment platform, where I own full-stack changes across our Assets platform — Terraform-provisioned IAM, environment promotions across dev, UAT, and prod on GCP, and Airflow orchestration with failure logging.

Before that, I spent two years at Red Hat working on OpenShift, leading automation for a live production migration from SDN to OVN-Kubernetes, building EgressIP and multicast automation in Go, rewriting our Jenkins end-to-end test framework, and overhauling our observability stack with Prometheus and an EFK logging pipeline. I hold a CKAD certification from that work. Earlier still, at Arm, I built Python automation that turned raw hardware architecture data into structured specs and documentation.

I hold a BS in Computer Engineering from the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign.
